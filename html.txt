  <div id="directoryAbout" v-if="isAboutActive &amp;&amp; !searchTerm" v-cloak="">
    <h4>Who is included in the MyDenison Campus Directory?</h4>
    <p>All faculty with phone numbers, full-time staff with phone numbers, and
      active students of the Denison community.</p>

    <h4>Can I change my directory information?</h4>
    <p>Yes! You can show or hide items in your directory profile, including your
      photo. <a v-on:click="tab='edit'">Edit your profile now</a> to adjust what
      information shows when others search for you. If your directory
      information is incorrect or needs to be updated:
    </p>
    <p><strong>Students</strong> should email Student Housing at <a href="mailto:housing@denison.edu?Subject=Profile%20Update">housing@denison.edu</a>.
    </p>
    <p><strong>Faculty and Staff</strong> should email the Office of Human
      Resources at <a href="mailto:humanresources@denison.edu?Subject=Profile%20Update">humanresources@denison.edu</a>.
    </p>

    <h4>Where did my photo come from and how can I change it?</h4>
    <p>The digital photograph in the MyDenison Campus Directory is your Denison ID
    Card photo. ID photos are submitted twice; once when you obtain your first
    Denison ID and again when you obtain your Blue Denison ID. ID photos are
    submitted online via the <a href="/node/7111">ID Request page</a>.
    Should you have any questions you may email
    <a href="mailto:housing@denison.edu">housing@denison.edu</a> or
    stop by the Student Housing Operations and Planning Office located in Doane
    102.</p>

    <p>Because the MyDenison Campus Directory serves Campus Security &amp; Safety,
    Health &amp; Counseling Services and other offices in locating and identifying
